My journey into programming and technology

Hello and Welcome to my portfolio, my name is Jesus Hernandez! I grew up in Chicago, IL, where my curiosity for technology started at an early age. I was always fascinated by how computers worked, from the hardware to the software that powers them. That curiosity quickly turned into a passion for programming, leading me to explore coding, software design, and web development in my free time.

In 2021, I enrolled at Purdue University Global to pursue a formal education in Information Technology with a concentration in Programming and Software Development. During my time there, I developed a strong foundation in front-end, web development and problem-solving with real-world applications. I graduated in 2024 with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, equipped with both technical skills and a drive to create innovative digital solutions.

Today, I continue to grow as a developer by building interactive experiences, learning new technologies, and contributing to projects that combine creativity with functionality. My journey is driven by a deep love for programming and a vision to use technology to make meaningful impact.
