My journey into programming and technology

Hello and Welcome to my portfolio, my name is Jesus Hernandez! I grew up in Chicago, IL, where my curiosity for technology started at an early age. I was always fascinated by how computers worked, from the hardware to the software that powers them. That curiosity quickly turned into a passion for programming, leading me to explore coding, software design, and web development in my free time.

In 2021, I enrolled at Purdue University Global to pursue a formal education in Information Technology with a concentration in Programming and Software Development. During my time there, I developed a strong foundation in front-end, web development and problem-solving with real-world applications.

I graduated in 2024 with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, equipped with both technical skills and a drive to create innovative digital solutions. Today, I continue to grow as a developer by building interactive experiences, learning new technologies, and contributing to projects that combine creativity with functionality. My journey is driven by a deep love for programming and a vision to use technology to make meaningful impact.

Breach-House

Breach-House is an interactive cyber attack tracker built as a collaborative project with a team of developers. Designed to visualize live and simulated breach data in a 3D environment, this experience immerses users in a futuristic security operations room. Developed using Three.js, GSAP, and layered HTML/CSS interfaces, the environment features real-time visualizations of attack paths, breach logs, and threat levels. Users can navigate through the scene using dynamic camera transitions triggered by clickable markers, explore interactive consoles, and engage with elements that simulate the flow of a cyber attack.

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Three.js, Blender, Vite, AWS Cloudfront, Python, Node.js

Narrify

Narrify is an accessibility-focused web application designed to turn written content into immersive audio experiences. Narrify allows users to upload PDFs and have the content read aloud in a clean, distraction free interface. This project combines file handling, text extraction, and text-to-speech technology, giving users a seamless way to engage with documents in audio form.
